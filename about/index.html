About us

Composed of an ethnically, culturally and gender-lyy diverse, comprehenisve and supportive team of white males aged 16/17, TG Enterprise was hailed amongst Britain's business community for their pioneering attitude towards Human Resources (HR).

The mystery of TG Enterprise's failure is one which still wracks the brains of many intelligent people to this very day, as business experts and historians try desparatly to untangle the twisted narrative. Suffice to say, it was not a glorius business.
