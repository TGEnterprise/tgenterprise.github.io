Welcome to our website!

TG Enterprise was founded on the 5th of October 2016, by Thomas George (aka TG), as well as his trusted board of directors. In December 2016, after a leadership challenge, Marley Sudbury became the Managing Director, a decision most people regret.

On the 1st of March 2017, after months of hardship, and with most members having jumped ship, TG Enterprise finally ceased to be. Most people didn't really care, continuing the trend they had followed since day one.
